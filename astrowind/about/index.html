About us

Built for Real Clinical Operations

HippoChat was created by people who understand the messy reality of cross-organizational clinical communication. We've seen the workarounds, the compliance gaps, and the frustration that comes from tools that weren't designed for how healthcare actually works.

Our Mission

Healthcare organizations need to communicate with external partners securely and accountably. But every existing "solution" either creates compliance risk, IT headaches, or both.

We built HippoChat to solve this specific problem: secure, auditable messaging that respects organizational boundaries while enabling the collaboration modern care requires.

What We Believe

The principles that guide how we build HippoChat.

  • 1

    Organizational boundaries matter

    External users shouldn't need access to your internal systems. Clear separation creates better security and clearer accountability.

  • 2

    Compliance should be built-in, not bolted on

    HIPAA compliance isn't a feature you add later. It needs to be in the architecture from day one.

  • 3

    Simple is better than complex

    If external partners need IT tickets and credentialing to communicate, they'll use SMS instead. Onboarding should take minutes, not weeks.

The Problem We Solve

Cross-organizational clinical communication is broken. Here's why existing solutions fail.

SMS and personal phones

Fast and convenient, but completely non-compliant. PHI on personal devices, no audit trail, no access controls.

Internal secure texting

Great for internal use, but giving external users access creates IT headaches and blurs organizational boundaries.

EHR access

"Just give them an account" creates permanent access, credentialing delays, and accounts that never get deprovisioned.

Phone and pager systems

No documentation, no audit trail, messages get lost or misheard. Callbacks create more problems than they solve.
