Features

Built for cross-organizational healthcare. Secure messaging that bridges the gap between internal systems and external partners.

  • Cross-Org Separation

    Each organization maintains its own users and boundaries. External users never touch your internal systems.

  • Secure Messaging

    HIPAA-aligned architecture with complete audit trails and message controls built in from day one.

  • Role-Based Access

    Fine-grained permissions ensure the right people see the right information at the right time.

  • Simple Onboarding

    Get external clinicians messaging in minutes, not weeks. No complex IAM integration required.

  • Retention Policies

    Set message retention rules that match your compliance requirements. Full control over data lifecycle.

  • BAA Available

    Business Associate Agreements available for HIPAA compliance. Built specifically for healthcare.
