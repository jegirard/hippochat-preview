Who It's For

Built for the people responsible when messaging fails. Designed for operators, clinical leaders, and compliance teams—not consumer chat.

Clinical Leadership

CMOs, Medical Directors, and Clinical Operations leaders who need reliable communication with external clinical partners.

What matters to you:

  • Care coordination that doesn't break compliance
  • Documentation for quality review
  • Accountability when issues arise

Operations / Admin

Practice managers, Operations Directors, and Administrative leaders who manage external vendor relationships.

What matters to you:

  • Simple onboarding for external partners
  • No IT tickets for every new user
  • Clear audit trails for disputes

CIO / IT

Technology leaders who need to enable clinical communication without compromising system security.

What matters to you:

  • No EHR integration headaches
  • External users stay outside internal systems
  • SaaS deployment with proper tenant isolation

Compliance / Security

Compliance Officers, Privacy Officers, and Security leaders who need to ensure HIPAA compliance for cross-org communication.

What matters to you:

  • HIPAA-aligned architecture
  • BAA available for every deployment
  • Complete audit logging
