Security & Compliance

HIPAA-aligned messaging without system entanglement. Security comes from clear boundaries—not from forcing everyone into the same system.

Compliance Posture

HIPAA-Aligned Architecture

Built from the ground up for HIPAA compliance. Not a consumer app with a compliance layer bolted on.

BAAs Available

Business Associate Agreements available for all deployments. Standard BAA terms or custom agreements as needed.

Role-Based Access

Fine-grained permissions that match clinical workflows. Users see what they need to see—nothing more.

Audit Logging

Complete audit trail for every message. Who sent what, when, to whom. Exportable for compliance review.

Retention Policies

Configurable retention policies per organization or channel. Meet your compliance requirements without manual intervention.

Encryption

Data encrypted in transit and at rest. TLS 1.3 for all communications. Industry-standard encryption at rest.

Why This Is Safer Than SMS

Every healthcare organization knows SMS is a compliance risk. But convenience often wins over compliance. Here's why HippoChat changes that equation:

SMS Reality

  • PHI stored on personal devices
  • No access controls
  • Messages persist indefinitely
  • No audit trail
  • Interceptable in transit

HippoChat

  • PHI stays in controlled environment
  • Role-based permissions
  • Configurable retention policies
  • Complete audit logging
  • TLS 1.3 encryption
