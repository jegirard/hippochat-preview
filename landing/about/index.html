About HippoChat

Built for real clinical operations. Not another consumer chat app wrapped in HIPAA claims.

HippoChat was built to solve a specific operational problem: secure communication between healthcare organizations that do not—and should not—share systems.

No platform sprawl. No consumer chat framing. No shortcuts around compliance.

Why We Exist

Healthcare IT is full of messaging tools—secure texting, EHR-integrated chat, patient portals, consumer apps with "HIPAA compliance." The problem? Almost all assume a single organization.

But healthcare doesn't work that way:

  • Triage nurses communicate with dozens of physician clients
  • Answering services handle calls for multiple practices
  • Coverage arrangements cross organizational boundaries every day

The workarounds—SMS, personal phones, "temporary" EHR access—create compliance gaps and operational headaches. They work until they don't, and then someone's explaining to auditors why PHI was on a personal iPhone.

What We Build

"The missing layer between internal secure messaging and consumer messaging."

HippoChat is purpose-built for cross-organizational clinical communication. Internal secure messaging assumes everyone shares systems. Consumer messaging ignores healthcare compliance entirely. We fill the gap.

Our focus: enabling secure, auditable communication between healthcare professionals who work together but don't—and shouldn't—share systems.

Our Principles

  • Focused scope. We solve cross-org messaging. We don't try to replace your EHR, your internal secure texting, or your patient portal.
  • Compliance by design. HIPAA alignment is built into the architecture, not bolted on afterward.
  • Organizational boundaries matter. External users should be clearly separated from internal systems. Different organizations, different access, different audit trails.
  • Simple deployment. SaaS delivery means you can be live in days, not months. No integration projects required.
