About HippoChat

Built for Real Clinical Operations

Our Mission

HippoChat was built to solve a real problem in healthcare: how do clinical teams communicate securely when they don't share the same systems?

Every day, nurses, physicians, and healthcare administrators work across organizational boundaries. Nurse triage services communicate with dozens of physician practices. On-call providers need to coordinate with staff at facilities where they don't have permanent access. Answering services relay critical messages between patients and providers at multiple organizations.

The existing solutions—SMS, pagers, giving external users access to internal systems—all create compliance risks, security vulnerabilities, and operational headaches.

HippoChat provides a purpose-built alternative: HIPAA-compliant messaging that maintains clear organizational boundaries while enabling the cross-organization communication that modern healthcare requires.

What We Believe

Security Through Separation

The safest architecture keeps external users external. You shouldn't have to grant access to internal systems just to send a secure message.

Compliance by Design

HIPAA compliance shouldn't depend on user behavior. The platform should make compliant communication the default, not an afterthought.

Built for Operators

We build for the people who are responsible when things go wrong—the operations leaders, compliance officers, and IT teams who need visibility and control.

Simple Over Complex

Healthcare already has enough complexity. We focus on solving the cross-org communication problem well, not building features you don't need.
