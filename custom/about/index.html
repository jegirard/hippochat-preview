Built for Real Clinical Operations

HippoChat was built to solve a specific operational problem: secure communication between healthcare organizations that do not—and should not—share systems.

No platform sprawl. No consumer chat framing. No shortcuts around compliance.

Our Focus

We focus exclusively on the cross-organizational communication problem in healthcare. This isn't a general-purpose messaging tool adapted for healthcare—it's purpose-built for the specific challenge of secure communication across organizational boundaries.

That focus means we understand the nuances: the compliance requirements, the operational realities, and the reasons why existing solutions fall short.

What We Believe

  • → Cross-organizational care is the norm, not the exception
  • → Security comes from clear boundaries, not system sprawl
  • → Compliance shouldn't require compromising on usability
  • → Healthcare deserves tools built for healthcare
