Messaging Built for Cross-Organizational Care

Most secure messaging tools assume everyone belongs to one organization. Healthcare doesn't work that way.

What HippoChat Is

HippoChat is a HIPAA-compliant messaging platform designed specifically for clinical communication that crosses organizational boundaries. It provides secure, auditable messaging between your team and external clinicians—without compromising your internal systems or creating compliance gaps.

Whether you're working with nurse triage services, answering services, or on-call coverage teams, HippoChat maintains clear boundaries while enabling the fast, context-rich communication that modern healthcare demands.

Core Capabilities

Cross-Organization User Separation

Each organization maintains its own users and boundaries. External users never touch your internal systems.

Secure Auditable Messaging

Complete audit trails and message controls built in from day one. Every message is tracked and accountable.

Role-Based Access Controls

Fine-grained permissions ensure the right people see the right information at the right time.

Configurable Retention Policies

Set message retention rules that match your compliance requirements. Full control over data lifecycle.

Simple Onboarding for External Users

Get external clinicians messaging in minutes, not weeks. No complex IAM integration required.

SaaS Multi-Tenant Deployment

Cloud-hosted with proper tenant isolation. No infrastructure to maintain on your end.

What It Replaces

SMS and Personal Phones

No audit trail, no compliance, no accountability.

Pager Systems

One-way communication with no context or response tracking.

Secure Texting Tools (Single-Org)

Built for one org, fail when care crosses boundaries.

"Temporary" Access to Internal Systems

Security risk that's never actually temporary.
