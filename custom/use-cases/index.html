When Clinical Teams Don't Share Systems

HippoChat exists for the moments when care crosses organizational boundaries.

Nurse Triage Services

External nurse triage teams need to communicate with your on-call providers, but granting them access to your internal systems creates security and compliance risks.

With HippoChat:

  • Triage nurses message providers directly with full clinical context
  • Complete audit trail of every communication
  • No access to your EHR or internal systems required
  • Clear organizational boundaries maintained

Answering Services

After-hours calls come through your answering service, but connecting those operators to on-call providers securely has always been a challenge.

With HippoChat:

  • Answering service staff reach on-call providers through secure messaging
  • Message history provides context and accountability
  • Replaces SMS and personal phone workarounds
  • HIPAA-compliant from the first message

On-Call Coverage Teams

When on-call coverage spans multiple organizations—locum providers, partner clinics, or coverage groups—communication becomes fragmented and hard to track.

With HippoChat:

  • Unified messaging across organizational boundaries
  • Clear accountability for who said what and when
  • No need to grant external providers access to internal systems
  • Easy onboarding for temporary or rotating coverage
