Our Security Philosophy

Many "secure messaging" solutions work by bringing everyone into the same system. This creates a larger attack surface and blurs organizational boundaries.

HippoChat takes a different approach: security through separation. External users communicate through HippoChat without ever accessing your internal systems, EHR, or secure texting platform.

This means fewer credentials to manage, clearer audit trails, and less risk when external relationships end.
