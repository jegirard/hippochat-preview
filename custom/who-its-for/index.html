Built for the People Responsible When Messaging Fails

Designed for operators, clinical leaders, and compliance teams—not consumer chat.

Clinical Leadership

You're responsible for clinical operations and patient outcomes. When communication breaks down between your team and external clinicians, it affects care quality.

HippoChat helps you:

  • Ensure clinical messages are tracked and accountable
  • Reduce communication delays with external partners
  • Maintain visibility into cross-org workflows

Operations / Admin

You manage the systems and processes that keep clinical operations running. When external partners need access, you're the one who has to figure out how.

HippoChat helps you:

  • Onboard external users in minutes, not weeks
  • Avoid complex IAM integrations for external access
  • Reduce the overhead of managing external relationships

CIO / IT

You're asked to enable external access while maintaining security. "Just give them access" is a request you hear too often, and it's never as simple as it sounds.

HippoChat helps you:

  • Keep external users out of internal systems entirely
  • Deploy SaaS without infrastructure overhead
  • Say yes to the business without creating security debt

Compliance / Security

You worry about SMS, personal phones, and "temporary" access that becomes permanent. Every workaround is a compliance risk waiting to be discovered.

HippoChat helps you:

  • Replace SMS workarounds with auditable messaging
  • Enforce retention policies across all communication
  • Provide clear audit trails for regulatory requirements
